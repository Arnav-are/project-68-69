English Elocution Program

The Multipurpose Activity Hall of Chinar Public School was the witness of Attitute, Confidence and Knowledge,when young orators from different schools and different regions of Alwar district appeared to participate in Interschool Elocution Competition held on 10.5.19 in the memory of honourable founder of Chinar Public School Late Shri S.S.N.Ganju,sir. It was really amazing to hear and see the contestants exhibiting their leadership skills on the same platform with different but with their best motions and notions on the topic ‘Artificial Intelligence- A way forward’ The programme began with the Chinar’s old tradition lighting the lamp and Ganesh Vandana in the form of classical dance. All the participants not only performed exceptionally well but also raised a question to think over,gave suggestions to find better solutions and came up with the ideas to move further for betterment of mankind. Our Presentation Gurus Mrs. Charu Sachdev and Mrs Preeti Ahuja encouraged,motivated and made the participants feel free to boost their morals.It made the event even more successful and meaningful. They offered some corrections to the participants as well as their mentors for further improvements and to add four moons to their success.
